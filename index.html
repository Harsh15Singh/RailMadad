Rail MADAD Grievance Redressal System

MADAD Grievance Redressal System is an online web-enabled system developed to redress complaints or suggestions.

MADAD Grievance Redressal System primarily aims to enable submission of grievances by the aggrieved party from anywhere and anytime (24x7).

The aggrieved party can submit their grievances through various modes such as post/letter, call center and grievance portal.

Tracking grievances is facilitated on this portal through the system generated ABDM unique grievance tracking number.
